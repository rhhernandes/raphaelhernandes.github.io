About

Reporter and data analyst at Folha de S.Paulo, Brazil's leading newspaper. Data and tech specialist. Applies data skills to reporting and writes about technology, particularly on the connection between tech and society, focusing on artificial intelligence, cybersecurity and ethics. Has five years worth of experience helping lead the company's digital efforts, understanding user behavior and doing audience metrics analysis. Interested in trust in the media.

Skills include: Python, SQL, Tableau, Google Data Studio, Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics, Chartbeat, CrowdTangle, data analysis, writing, editing, communication and translation. Currently studying AI, PyTorch for deep learning.

Responsible for the project Privacidade para Jornalistas (Privacy for Journalists) in Brazil.
